About Mi Flash Tool

Mi Flash Tool is the official firmware flashing utility developed by Xiaomi for flashing MIUI firmware on Xiaomi, Redmi, and POCO devices. It's essential for installing stock firmware, unbricking devices, and downgrading/upgrading MIUI versions.

How to Use Mi Flash Tool

  1. Download Firmware

    Download the appropriate MIUI firmware for your Xiaomi device model

  2. Install Mi Flash Tool

    Download and install Mi Flash Tool with administrator privileges

  3. Enable Fastboot Mode

    Boot your device into Fastboot mode (Power + Volume Down)

  4. Connect Device

    Connect your Xiaomi device to PC via USB cable

  5. Load Firmware

    Select the firmware folder in Mi Flash Tool

  6. Start Flashing

    Click Flash button and wait for the process to complete

Important Warnings

  • Always backup your device before flashing firmware
  • Ensure you download the correct firmware for your exact device model
  • Keep the device connected throughout the flashing process
  • Flashing firmware will void your device warranty
  • Use original USB cable for stable connection
