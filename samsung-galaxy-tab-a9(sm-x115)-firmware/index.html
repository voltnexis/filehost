How to Flash Firmware

  1. Download Firmware

    Select and download the appropriate firmware for your region

  2. Install Odin Tool

    Download and install Samsung Odin flashing tool on your PC

  3. Enter Download Mode

    Power off tablet, then hold Volume Down + Power to enter Download Mode

  4. Connect to PC

    Connect tablet to PC via USB cable with Samsung drivers installed

  5. Load Firmware

    In Odin, load AP, BL, CP, CSC files from the downloaded firmware

  6. Start Flashing

    Click Start in Odin and wait for the green PASS message

Important Warning

  • Always backup your device before flashing firmware
  • Ensure you download the correct region firmware for your device
  • Do not disconnect the device during the flashing process
  • Flashing firmware will void your warranty
  • Use original USB cable for stable connection
