About VLC Media Player

VLC is a free and open source cross-platform multimedia player and framework that plays most multimedia files as well as DVDs, Audio CDs, VCDs, and various streaming protocols. Developed by the VideoLAN project, VLC has become the world's most popular media player with over 3 billion downloads.

Universal Playback

Plays virtually any media format without additional codecs

Cross-Platform

Available on Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, iOS

Network Streaming

Stream from network, capture devices, and online sources

Advanced Features

Subtitle support, audio/video filters, and customization

Supported Media Formats

VLC supports an extensive range of multimedia formats without requiring additional codec packs:

Video Formats

MP4, AVI, MKV, MOV, WMV, FLV, 3GP, WEBM, OGV

Audio Formats

MP3, FLAC, AAC, OGG, WMA, WAV, M4A, APE

Streaming Protocols

HTTP, HTTPS, FTP, MMS, RTSP, RTP, UDP

Disc Formats

DVD, Blu-ray, CD, VCD, SVCD

Subtitle Formats

SRT, ASS, SSA, VTT, SUB, IDX

Playlist Formats

M3U, PLS, XSPF, ASX, B4S
