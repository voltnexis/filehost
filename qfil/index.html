About QFIL Tool

QFIL (Qualcomm Flash Image Loader) is the official flashing tool developed by Qualcomm for flashing firmware on devices powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon processors. It's essential for flashing stock firmware, unbricking devices, and performing low-level operations on Qualcomm-based smartphones and tablets.
